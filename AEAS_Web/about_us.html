History in brief

Our company was founded by Irshad Ahmad, on 2015 after he quitted his job in Abu Dhabi.

He founded this company to realize his childhood dream of running his own business. His mission behind creating this firm was to provide world class services in the electrical and automation industries.

Since 2015, we have provided products and services to many clients like CCL Rajrappa, TATA Steel West Bokaro, NKCPL Ghatotand and Jamadoba and many more.

We have actively provided training courses to the young minds in colleges and universities. These institutes are RTC Institute of Technology, Cambridge Institute of Technology, BIT Mesra and GGS Bokaro.

Our firm has a sister company called 'Mariyam Construction'. This firm mostly deals in Civil related work.
